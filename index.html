Little knowledge about oil painting

History about Oil painting

1.The predecessor of oil painting was the tempera in European painting before the 15th century.  

2.In modern oil paintings, linseed oil blending pigments are used to paint on treated cloth or wood boards. Because oil paints do not change color after drying, the various colors are not dirty, and the painter can draw rich and realistic colors.

Painti1ng tools

  • Video about oil painting basics tools

    Click the Video to know more about the painting tools in oil painting

  • Painting paper - Canvas

    1. The standard canvas, is made by stretching the linen or canvas on the wooden inner frame, blending it with glue or oil and white powder and painting it on the surface of the cloth.

    2.The thickness of the cloth is determined by the size of the frame and is also selected according to the needs of the painting effect.

  • Washing bucket

    Washing bucket can make the brushes clean. Wash the brush when you need during or after the painting.

  • Pigments

    1. Most of the original pigments were mineral pigments, which were hand-milled into fine powders to reconcile the nose during painting.

     

    2.The properties of a pigment are related to the chemical composition it contains. When tinted, the chemical action can cause adverse reactions between some pigments.

     

    3.I think pigments play the most important part in all painting, because it can make the works colorful.

  • Painting Brush -Noraml

    1.The brush is made of elastic animal hair and has a rounded shape, a flat front, a short front and a fan shape.

     

    2.I often use five brushes,including big and small brushes.

  • Painting Brush - Fan-shaped pen

    1. It is a new type of special oil brush.

     

    2.It is used for swiping and brushing in wet painting, or softening the outline with too sharp.（such as：leaves of the tree）

     

    3.But i used normal brush all the time.

  • Oil painting's knife

    (This is a works drawing buy oil knife.)

     

    1.The oil knife has many different shapes. The shape of the blade is round or square, or pointed, and even has a zigzag edge. But no matter how the knife shape changes, there are three types of functions: palette knife, drawing knife and scraper.

     

    2.Different using style of the knife:

          1. Drawing knife:Painting with a knife can achieve the effect that the brush is difficult to achieve.                                                       2. Palette knife: Used to blend oil paints on a palette or plate.                                                    3.scraper: Mainly used to clean up the waste paint on the palette.

  • Sketchpad

    You can mix the pigments together to get another color on the sketchpad.

  • Turpentine（松節油） & Varnish（上光油）

    (I use two situation to tell you the different between using or don't use the urpentine & Varnish)

     

    1.Turpentine：It acts as a diluent pigment and make the works dry more faster and have a brighter colors.

     

    2.I often use five brushes,including big and small brushes.

     

    3.I often use turpentine and varnish together.
